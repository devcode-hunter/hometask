Revolut - IT Administrator Home Task

My bluetooth keyboard ain't connectin' :(

1. Check Keyboard Power

  • 1) Ensure your keyboard is turned on.
  • 2) Check if the keyboard has sufficient battery or is properly charged.

2. Turn Bluetooth Off and On

  • 1) Go to System Settings > Bluetooth.
  • 2) Toggle Bluetooth off, wait a few seconds, and then toggle it back on.

3. Remove and Re-add Keyboard

  • 1) In System Settings > Bluetooth, find your keyboard in the list of devices.
  • 2) Click the "x" next to the keyboard to remove it.
  • 3) Put your keyboard in pairing mode and add it again.

4. Restart Your Mac

  • 1) Click the Apple logo in the top-left corner.
  • 2) Select Restart and wait for your Mac to reboot.
  • 3) Try connecting your keyboard again after the restart.

5. Reset Bluetooth Module

  • 1) Hold Shift + Option and click the Bluetooth icon in the menu bar.
  • 2) Select Reset the Bluetooth module from the dropdown menu.
  • 3) Confirm the reset and try reconnecting your keyboard.

6. Update macOS

  • 1) Go to System Settings > Software Update.
  • 2) Ensure your Mac is running the latest version of macOS (Sonoma *hint hint*).
  • 3) Install any available updates and try connecting your keyboard again.
