I can't load anything in Chrome :(

1. Check Internet Connection

  • 1) Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • 2) Switch off your Wi-Fi and try connecting again. Sometimes the Wi-Fi just needs some tender loving care.

2. Clear Cache and Cookies

  • 1) Go to chrome://settings/clearBrowserData in Chrome.
  • 2) Select "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files."
  • 3) Click "Clear data." and see if it works.

3. Disable Extensions

  • 1) Type chrome://extensions/ in the address bar.
  • 2) Disable all extensions by toggling them off.
  • 3) Try loading the pages again to see if an extension is causing the issue.

4. Update Chrome

  • 1) Click on the three dots in the top-right corner of Chrome.
  • 2) Go to Help > About Google Chrome.
  • 3) Update to the latest version, restart Chrome and check if it works.

5. Reset Chrome Settings

  • 1) Go to chrome://settings/reset.
  • 2) Click "Restore settings to their original defaults."
  • 3) Confirm by clicking "Reset settings."

6. Check VPN Settings

  • 1) Try turning off your AWS VPN (or any VPN that is connected) and see if the website loads.

7. Try Incognito Mode

  • 1) Open a new incognito window by pressing Cmd+Shift+N (Mac).
  • 2) Try loading the pages to see if the issue persists.

8. Check Proxy Settings

  • 1) Go to chrome://settings/system.
  • 2) Click "Open your computer's proxy settings."
  • 3) Ensure that no unwanted proxy settings are enabled.

9. Reinstall Chrome

  • 1) Uninstall Chrome from your computer.
  • 2) Download the latest version from the official website.
  • 3) Install Chrome and check if the issue is resolved.
